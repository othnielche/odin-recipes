Eru

Ingredients

  • Eru
  • Palm Oil
  • Dry Fish
  • Cray Fish
  • Pepper
  • Canda (Cow hyde)
  • Meat
  • Water Leaf

Steps

  1. Place the meat and Fish to boil
  2. Wash water leaf and strain in a collender.
  3. Add strained water leaf to boiling meat (NB: very little water)
  4. Wash eru and strain in a collendar
  5. Add strained eru to pot and leave to simmer.
  6. After sometime when eru begins to reduce add the palm oil to it and stir with a spatula.
  7. Add the grounded pepper to the mix for flavor.
